## Notes

- **NC clause**: Smarthistory and World History Encyclopedia content is licensed non-commercial. Fine for a demo/educational build; revisit before any commercial release (paraphrased facts themselves are not copyrightable, but keep verbatim passages out of a commercial build or replace these two sources).
- **Wikipedia** was used as scaffolding during initial drafting (2026-07-09) and fully removed on 2026-07-10 (editorial decision: institutional and specialist publishers only). Every chunk was re-verified against the sources above; facts supported only by Wikipedia were deleted rather than kept.
- **Britannica** was consulted for cross-checking only; nothing ingested.
- Attribution lives in the repository README (§Attribution): the terrain credit line (mandatory, CC BY 4.0) plus a "Historical information drawn from:" list of the sources above.
